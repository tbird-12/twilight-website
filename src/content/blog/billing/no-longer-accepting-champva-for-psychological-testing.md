Effective **May 19, 2026**, Twilight Psychology is **no longer accepting CHAMPVA as the primary insurance** for **psychological testing** services. This change applies immediately.

We will **continue to accept CHAMPVA as secondary insurance**, and we will **continue accepting CHAMPVA for [therapy](/services/therapy) and [medication management](/services/medication-management)**.

## What is changing

This update applies to psychological testing claims that rely on CHAMPVA as the **primary payer**, including the testing administration codes **96136** and **96137** discussed in our post on [common CPT codes used in psychological testing](/blog/billing/common-cpt-codes-used-in-psychological-testing).

We are making this change because CHAMPVA has not paid these psychological testing codes on a timely basis, and the administrative burden has become unsustainable for a small practice.

## Why Twilight Psychology made this decision

Over time, our team has faced several recurring problems when trying to bill CHAMPVA for psychological testing:

- **Untimely non-payment** of codes **96136** and **96137**
- **No workable prior authorization link for providers** when testing authorization is needed
- **Extensive administrative follow-up** for claims that already require substantial staff time
- **Extensive documentation requirements** that still often end in denial

As a practice, we cannot continue carrying the financial and administrative burden created by these requirements. We will cease to accept CHAMPVA as primary coverage for psychological testing.

## Important billing note

Twilight Psychology and other private practices do **not** have a participation contract with CHAMPVA. At the same time, this is **not** a situation where we can simply bill testing to CHAMPVA, wait for non-payment or denial, and then solve the problem through balance billing.

In practical terms, that means CHAMPVA's billing rules do not create a workable path for us to keep offering psychological testing under CHAMPVA as a primary plan. Rather than place families and our practice in an unclear or financially unstable position, we are setting this policy clearly in advance.

## What CHAMPVA clients can still use at Twilight

We will continue accepting CHAMPVA in these situations:

- As **secondary insurance**
- For **[therapy](/services/therapy)**
- For **[medication management](/services/medication-management)**

If you are seeking a [psychological evaluation](/services/psychological-evaluations) and CHAMPVA is your primary plan, please review our [fees and insurance page](/resources/fees) for self-pay options and contact us before scheduling so we can help you review next steps.

## We are sorry for the inconvenience

We know this change may be frustrating, especially for families who have been trying to access psychological testing through their benefits. We are sorry for the inconvenience and appreciate your understanding.
