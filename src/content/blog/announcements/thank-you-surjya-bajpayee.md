Today is **Surjya Bajpayee's last day** at Twilight Psychology, and we want to take a moment to thank her for the time, care, and heart she has shared with our practice.

Surjya has been with Twilight Psychology for **two semesters** as a practicum student. During that time, she brought a thoughtful presence to our team and a genuine commitment to the people and work around her.

We are deeply grateful for the energy and compassion she invested in this season with us. Practicum experiences are shaped not only by learning, but also by the relationships, professionalism, and perspective each student brings, and Surjya contributed all of that in meaningful ways.

As she finishes this chapter, we want to recognize both her work and the care she showed throughout her time here. We are thankful she spent these two semesters with Twilight Psychology, and we wish her the very best in what comes next.

Thank you, Surjya.
