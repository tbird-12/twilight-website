## Our Commitment Remains Unchanged

At Twilight Psychology, our commitment to providing high-quality, compassionate mental health services to the Kentucky community remains our highest priority. We're writing to share important information about how we're adjusting our operations to ensure we remain a stable, accessible resource for years to come.

## The Challenge: A Perfect Storm

In June 2026, the Kentucky Department for Medicaid Services announced a 4% reduction in provider reimbursement rates, effective August 1, 2026. Like many healthcare providers across the Commonwealth, this presents a significant challenge to our sustainability.

But this cut is only part of a larger financial squeeze. Psychological testing services—our core specialty—have been hit especially hard:

- **Stagnant reimbursement**: The CPT codes for psychological testing (90791, 96136, 96137, 96130, 96131) have not seen a rate increase since 2018
- **Unequal treatment**: Therapy services received Medicaid rate increases on April 1, 2026, but psychological testing was excluded
- **Rising costs**: Testing supplies from providers like Pearson and WPS have increased significantly in cost
- **Resource intensity**: Comprehensive evaluations require substantial clinician time, specialized expertise, and careful documentation

We are one of the few practices in Kentucky that conducts rigorous comprehensive evaluations and accepts insurance for this service. For years, we absorbed these growing costs to serve Medicaid patients. However, we cannot continue this approach without jeopardizing the quality of evaluations or the long-term health of our practice.

## Our Solution: A Balanced Approach

To remain a stable, sustainable resource for the Kentucky community, we are thoughtfully adjusting our service capacity. This shift is designed to protect both the quality of our work and our ability to continue serving Medicaid patients long-term.

### What's Changing
We are prioritizing the depth and quality of every evaluation we conduct. This means adjusting how many comprehensive psychological tests we perform at any given time, allowing us to dedicate proper resources to each patient's diagnostic process.

### What This Means for You
- **Higher-quality evaluations**: Longer wait times ensure we can provide rigorous assessments, thorough interpretation, and detailed, clinically useful reports
- **Sustained access**: By balancing our services, we are protecting our ability to continue serving Kentucky Medicaid members and accepting insurance
- **Transparent pricing**: If you prefer a faster timeline, [self-pay options are available](/resources/fees)

You can [view our current waitlist times](/resources/waitlist-times) to help plan your care accordingly.

### Our Core Commitment
We are not closing our doors or abandoning Medicaid patients. We are making a proactive choice to ensure that Twilight Psychology remains fiscally healthy and able to provide the highest standard of care for years to come.

## We're Here to Help

We understand that service adjustments can create uncertainty. This decision reflects our deep commitment to transparency and ensuring the long-term wellbeing of our patients and the Kentucky community.

If you have questions about how this affects your care, coverage, or next steps, please reach out. We're here to help you navigate this transition.
