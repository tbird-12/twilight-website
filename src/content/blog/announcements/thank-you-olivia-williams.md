We want to take a moment to thank **Olivia Williams, PhD, LPA** for her time and contribution to Twilight Psychology.

Since **September 2025**, Olivia stepped in on a need basis — under the supervision of **Dr. Heather Cornett** — to help meet the growing demand for neurodivergent evaluations at our practice. She conducted psychological assessments for children ages 2 and above, and her willingness to show up when families needed answers made a real difference.

Olivia has maintained her own private practice throughout her time with us, and she has made the decision to focus her energy there going forward. We respect that choice and are glad she shared this season of her work with us.

We are grateful for the clinical care and professionalism she brought to every evaluation here. The families she assessed were well served by her thoroughness and commitment.

Thank you, Olivia. We wish you continued success in your practice.
