We are pleased to announce that **Twilight Psychology is now in-network with Wellcare of Kentucky** (also known as WellCare Health Plans of Kentucky). Effective immediately, we are accepting new clients who are covered under this plan for both **therapy** and **medication management** services.

## What This Means for You

If you or a family member are enrolled in Wellcare of Kentucky, you can now schedule services at Twilight Psychology using your Wellcare benefits. As an in-network provider, your visits will be billed directly to Wellcare at contracted rates, which typically means lower out-of-pocket costs compared to using an out-of-network provider.

## Services Now Available Under Wellcare of Kentucky

- **Therapy** — Individual therapy sessions with our licensed clinicians.
- **Medication Management** — Psychiatric medication evaluation and ongoing management for conditions such as ADHD, depression, anxiety, and related concerns.

> **Note:** We are not accepting new clients for Psychological testing (e.g., autism and ADHD evaluations) who have Wellcare of KY Medicaid due to the long waitlists. We are able to provide referrals to other agencies who have shorter waitlists and provide the necessary medical services sooner.

## How to Get Started

If you are interested in scheduling an appointment, please:

1. Visit our [New Client](/resources/new-client) page for intake information.
2. Contact our office directly by phone or through our [Contact](/contact) page.
3. Have your Wellcare of Kentucky insurance card available when you reach out — we will verify your benefits as part of the intake process.
