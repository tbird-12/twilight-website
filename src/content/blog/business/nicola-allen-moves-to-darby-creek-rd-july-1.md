Starting **July 1, 2026**, **Nicola Allen, MSN, PMHNP-BC** will begin seeing medication management clients at **503 Darby Creek Rd., Suite B** in Lexington. Her appointments will be moving from her previous Lexington location to the new Darby Creek office.

## What is changing

Beginning July 1, Nicola's in-person medication management appointments will take place at **503 Darby Creek Rd., Suite B**. The change is part of our continued growth at the Lexington office and helps keep medication management and evaluation services organized under the same clinical space.

## What clients should know

- Please use **503 Darby Creek Rd., Suite B** for any appointments scheduled with Nicola starting on **July 1, 2026**.
- If you have an upcoming appointment with Nicola, please review your reminder or contact our office if you have questions about the new location.
- Telehealth visits remain available for eligible clients, and the same scheduling process will continue for new and returning patients.
