We are pleased to welcome **Ethan Puckett, M.A., T-LPA** to Twilight Psychology. Ethan joins the practice on **July 6, 2026** and will provide **neuro-affirming therapy for children and adolescents ages 3 to 18**, with his strongest clinical fit typically in the **7 to 16** age range.

He will also provide **psychological testing** and **psychoeducational testing** for families who need more clarity around learning, attention, autism, and emotional-behavioral concerns.

## Therapy services with Ethan

Ethan's therapy work is especially well-suited for children and adolescents who need support with Autism, ADHD, Anxiety, Depression, Trauma, Behavioral concerns
and School-related stress.

He uses **behavioral therapy**, **Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)**, and **Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT)** with a person-centered, strengths-oriented approach.

## Older teen therapy options starting July 6

Beginning **July 6**, Ethan will be **accepting new therapy clients with no waitlist**. In addition to in-person care, he will also offer **virtual therapy sessions for older teens**.

This gives families another flexible option when transportation, school schedules, or comfort with telehealth are part of the treatment planning conversation.

## Ethan's background and training

Ethan graduated with his **Master's in Applied Psychology from Western Kentucky University** and is on track to graduate with his **PsyD in Clinical Psychology from Western Kentucky University in August 2026**.

He is a **temporarily licensed psychological associate** practicing under the supervision of **Dr. Heather Cornett**.

Before joining Twilight Psychology, Ethan worked as an outpatient therapist in a community mental health center, where he completed weekly therapy sessions and autism evaluations. He also most recently worked as a **school psychologist intern**, completing many school-based evaluations for learning disabilities, autism, ADHD, and other cognitive, emotional, and behavioral concerns.

## Testing services Ethan offers

In addition to therapy, Ethan's work at Twilight Psychology includes:

- Psychological testing
- Psychoeducational testing

These services may be helpful when a child, teen, family, or school team needs a clearer understanding of attention, learning, neurodivergence, or emotional-behavioral functioning.
