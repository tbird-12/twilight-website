Taylor Pennington, MS, T-LPA joins Twilight Psychology as a temporarily licensed psychological associate supervised by Dr. Heather Cornett. Taylor holds a master's degree in counseling psychology from the University of Louisville and brings experience in school-based therapy and specialized outpatient services for youth with autism, ADHD, and cognitive delays.

Taylor provides psychological and psychoeducational testing (including evaluations for intellectual disability, autism spectrum disorder, and ADHD) and offers relational-cultural therapy for clients aged 5–16. Taylor is passionate about supporting families after a neurodivergent diagnosis, advocating for children in school settings, and translating assessment findings into practical recommendations.

Taylor's first clinical date with Twilight Psychology is August 10, 2026. Availability will be:

- Monday: 9:00–16:00 (virtual)
- Tuesday: 9:00–16:00 (virtual)
- Wednesday: 8:00–17:00 (in person)
- Thursday: 8:00–16:00 (in person)
- Friday: 9:00–15:00 (virtual)

To learn more about Taylor's services or to request an appointment starting August 10, 2026, visit Taylor's clinician profile: [/about/clinicians/taylor-pennington](/about/clinicians/taylor-pennington).

We welcome Taylor to the team and look forward to the thoughtful, family-centered care they bring to our clinic.
